How It Works

  1. Select the home you'd like to visit.
  2. Sign in or create an account. Select a day and time to tour the home.
  3. Verify your identity with your driver's license or credit card information.*
  4. Receive a digital code to unlock the front door of the home. Your code will expire in one hour after your scheduled tour time

*For identification purposes only, ID verification required only once, upon your initial registration
