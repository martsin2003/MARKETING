Your family will be immediately at home once they step inside the spacious and stylish Beckner. It's got room for everyone and all your favorite things. You'll open the door into a grand foyer with a formal dining room or living room to your right. Follow the hall as the home opens up into the grand room, the breakfast area, and the kitchen. The open design means your family can stay connectd while you enjoy the home's most popular rooms.

The second level features four large bedrooms, but none more impressive than the owner's suite. It's complete with dual walk-in closets and a spacious bathroom with double vanities.

The lower level is what you make it. There's space for an oversized recreation room, a game room, or a place for a big-screen television and all your favorite movies.
